Smaller Class sizes In New York City Public Schools

By Evelin Fajardo-Alvarez

November 6, 2023

Smaller class sizes in New York City Public Schools has been a long term debate for several years.

In September of 2022, Governor Hochul signed a bill into effect that would cap class sizes of New York Public Schools. Kindergarten through third grade is capped at 20 students, grades four to eight at 23 students and High School classrooms at 25 students.

Educators and parents alike agree that smaller class sizes would greatly benefit the children.
